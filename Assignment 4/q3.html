The Art of Simple Cooking
By Sathya • August 1, 2025
Cooking isn't just a necessity—it's an expression of creativity, culture, and care. Whether you're crafting a gourmet dinner or a simple home meal, the process is a blend of art and science.
With the right techniques and ingredients, anyone can make delicious meals. It's not about complexity, but about balance—flavor, texture, and timing all come together in harmony.
Start with what you have, keep experimenting, and don’t be afraid to fail. Every dish is a story, and every cook is a storyteller.
